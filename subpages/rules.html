Rules

After ironing stand, it is possible forvinterroning.
This assumes that Aalborg Roklub's rules for winter rowing strictly observed.
There shall be no ice in the fjord.
There must be an experienced officer in the boat.
The crew must be experienced and of course released.
All must wear a life jacket.
There must be notified to a director before setting out.
Fjord must not be crossed, meaning that there be rowed in from Aalborg side and the shore.
Only rowed into the harbor - with the Eastern Harbour as a frontier
For OUTRIGGER is the limit though - Scandinavia's Wharf and the Eastern Harbour
It is only the boats that are made available for winter rowing, may be used. (See them on the bulletin board. For both coastal same rules apply as for Inrigger - for outrigger see below)
Outriggerroning should be in the same area as exercise rowing - but only when accompanied by speedboat.
