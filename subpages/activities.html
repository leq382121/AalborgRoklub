Activities

Rowing outdoors

Aalborg rowers has one of the best conditions in Denmark. Due to the wideness of Limfjord, it is safe for us to row and the risk to have a collision while rowing is low. It is great fun and challenge together to move the boat while pushing and pulling oaks out of the water.

Rowing indoors

Rowing indoors is important to keep your routine when due to frozen and/or cold water it is not possible to practice outside. For that we are using modern rowing ergometers named “Concept” where a coach is walking around and evaluates your technique which is important while rowing on the water when the season comes.

Other Activities

We are doing more than Indoor and outdoor rowing. We are Jogging every morning together, going to the swimming pool every Sunday and just being and spending time together in order to make a crew connection and strengthen the spirit of being a part of a team.
