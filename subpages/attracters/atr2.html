Indoor rowing

Rowing is good, but have you ever thought about rowers in winter? Do you think we eat nachos and drink diet coke not to get much fat until summer? Either if the answer is yes or no, you still might be interested. Half of autumn, spring and whole winter we still row. How is that possible? It is because we are using modern rowing Ergometers named "Concept". Concepts help us to keep our rowing technique in as high level as possible and develop it further. This is the part of rowing where coach is walking around you and analyzing, advising your rowing to make you feel better while making a stroke, keep your body in right position, make you able to breath correctly, not hurt your back and make the strongest strokes for the longest distance. Also, technique is very important on the water, because if you won’t do it correctly, you won’t be able to take your oaks from the water, make yourself slower and you will fall off from the boat. You really want to develop your rowing technique very well. It is not easy to learn rowing. It will require time and effort, but if you are going to manage it, you will be proud of yourself so much, that you won't be able to stop striving for excellence. Take a shot of your first kilometers ant start your rowing journey today.
