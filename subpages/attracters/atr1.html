Training In Aalborg Rowing club

Rowing consist of many elements, like boat, crew, type of the water source, but there is one important thing and it is training. Without training your muscles and stamina you cannot go for a long distance. If you master the technique, it is possible to row for a long time, but it is not about the time you can sit up in the boat. It is about how fast you can manage to row specific distance. In order to achieve the best result and to apply your strength to the technique, you need to have a good training. Aalborg rowing club is offering not only possibility to row on water and indoors, but also to train your muscles. We have our personal gym, where no one unknown is disturbing you with their smell, strange sounds and liquids. Aalborg rowing club has high and strict regulations about keeping clean after yourself, so you can have best training in a gym without any thought of getting an infection or just having a bad smell after someone. Also, quality of tools is important too. We are offering you a place to train 24/7, whatever, whenever you want.
