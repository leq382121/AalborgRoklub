Our Purpose

Mission To promote and develop Aalborg rowing experience and results in the northern Jutland area by adequate coaching and managing club and its rowers, spread the knowledge about rowing itself and strive for the excellence by achieving best results while applying most innovative and usable rowing development techniques. By this, Aalborg rowing club wants and have to provide care for every rower, invest into tools, humanistic factors and provide quality check for each training activities. Values

• Respectful - Club respects each member's choices and being tolerant is the main priority.

It has a lot of value, since Denmark is very adaptive and friendly country. This is one of the core values, why Denmark is one of the happiest countries in the world. Freedom of choice, being what you are, what you like to be.

• Rower-centered - Rowing club activities are concentrated to deliver the best experience and coaching for every member.

It’s all about the person. Coach have to be friends with everyone and being coach or worker in Aalborg rowing club id does not mean that you will only be doing it. Being a part of community means helping each other, it does not matter, either member need persona or professional help.

• Fun - Aalborg rowing club is doing its best to make rowing as much fun and interesting as possible.

No trainings are interested when you are not feeling happy or excited about it. Fun ads motivation and eager to go for trainings more and spend time together with crew and others.
